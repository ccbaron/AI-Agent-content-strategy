# Content Strategy 2026

## Main goal

Position the product as a content intelligence tool that combines strategy, research, and AI-assisted execution.

## Priorities

- Differentiate from generic LLM chat experiences.
- Show practical value in research, comparison, ideation, and rewriting.
- Communicate a more strategic experience, not just a generative one.

## Core messages

- It does not just generate content, it helps make better content decisions.
- It combines memory, external tools, and internal knowledge.
- It helps turn scattered information into actionable messaging.

## Recommended positioning angles

- Emphasize strategic clarity.
- Highlight speed with judgment.
- Avoid vague promises such as “revolutionize everything” or “transform your workflow instantly”.
