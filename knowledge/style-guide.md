# Style Guide

## Tone

- Clear
- Strategic
- Direct
- Professional
- Natural

## Avoid

- Excessive jargon
- Inflated phrasing
- Grandiose promises
- Overly generic language

## Prefer

- Concrete sentences
- Strong verbs
- Specific benefits
- Easy-to-scan structures

## Writing principles

- Prioritize clarity over cleverness.
- Reduce filler words.
- Show strategic implications when useful.
- Adapt the level of detail to the task.
